OUR STORY

Authentic Thai Cuisine Since 1995

Siam Orchid was founded by Chef Somchai after moving from Bangkok to bring authentic Thai flavors to your table. Our recipes are passed down through generations, using only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods.

Every dish is prepared with care, respecting traditional techniques while incorporating modern presentation to create a truly memorable dining experience.
